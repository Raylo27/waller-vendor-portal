# Demo Script

## Problem
The City of Waller does not appear to have a modern public-facing vendor portal with vendor self-registration, opportunity browsing, and digital submission flow.

## Demo Goal
Show a believable 10–15 minute pilot experience for both vendors and staff.

## Key Walkthrough
1. Homepage
2. Vendor registration
3. Registration confirmation
4. Opportunities list
5. Opportunity detail
6. Submission form
7. Submission confirmation
8. Staff dashboard
9. Post new opportunity
10. Vendor registry
11. Submissions review

## Most Important Moment
A new staff-posted opportunity appears on the vendor side immediately.
